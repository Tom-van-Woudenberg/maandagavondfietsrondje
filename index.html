Activities

Join us every Monday evening for exciting cycling activities in Delft. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced cyclist, we have something for everyone.

Club Rides

Join our club rides and explore the beautiful cycling routes in and around Delft. Whether you prefer road cycling, mountain biking, or leisurely rides, we have a ride for every cyclist.

How to Subscribe

To subscribe for our club rides, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website at www.cyclingcommunitydelft.com
  2. Create an account or log in if you already have one
  3. Navigate to the "Club Rides" section
  4. Select the ride you want to join
  5. Click on the "Subscribe" button
  6. Complete the registration form and submit
